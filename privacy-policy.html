Fibr Privacy & Security Policy

Fibr ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is stored and processed by the Fibr Chrome extension and how you remain in full control of your data.

Information We Collect

  • Gemini API Key: Stored securely in chrome.storage.local and never transmitted to any Fibr-controlled server.
  • No personal data: Fibr does not collect browsing history, personal info, or page content unless you explicitly choose to analyze it.

How We Use Information

  • Your API key solely authenticates requests that go directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API.
  • All AI processing occurs on Google infrastructure; Fibr does not proxy or inspect the requests.
  • Generated content (posts, threads, notes) can be saved locally to power the in-app gallery and history features.

Information Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • All API calls are client-side and communicate directly with Google Gemini.
  • No analytics, advertising beacons, or third-party trackers are embedded in the extension.

Data Security

  • Chrome's encrypted storage protects your API key; you can remove it anytime from the extension.
  • All traffic to Gemini uses HTTPS with TLS encryption end-to-end.
  • Wherever possible, data processing happens entirely inside your browser; only the Gemini completion requests leave the device.

User Rights & Controls

  • You may delete your API key and any saved content at any time via the extension settings.
  • You choose when to run content analysis; nothing is sent without user action.
  • For privacy questions or removal requests, email grow.with.fibr@gmail.com.
